Selenium script for automating Kobster's Elite portal
======================================================
- This is a simple script which automates the Ordering process.
- Products would be ordered through PURCHASE LIST followed by another order through QUICK BUY.

THINGS REQUIRED
---------------
- Eclipse IDE
- Selenium WebDriver
- Firefox/Chrome Browser
